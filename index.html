What is RuckStack?

RuckStack is a web application for planning trips with other people. Instead of juggling shared spreadsheets, group chats, and booking confirmation emails, everything lives in one place:

  • Plans — flights, hotels, activities, road trips, and package tours, grouped by day or status
  • Costs — track what everything costs with per-person splits and multi-currency support
  • Essentials — store insurance, visas, passes, and permits with document attachments
  • Ideas board — collect and vote on places to visit before committing them to the itinerary
  • Map — see all your plans plotted on a map to check distances and plan movement
  • Multi-traveler — add everyone going, assign plans per person, and track individual costs
